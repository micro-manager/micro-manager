Setting up Serial Ports

  • Here you will identify settings for the serial ports you use to control your devices.
  • The settings are shown at the right-hand side and the particlar settings for your device can also be found in the same Device Support pages on the Micro-Manager website.
  • If you are still unsure what the correct settings are, try: BaudRate=9600 baud, Parity=None, StopBits=1.
Note:
  • The AnswerTimeout parameter specifies (in milliseconds) how long Micro-Manager will wait for the response from the device.
  • Values from 500 ms to 2000 ms are OK for most devices, but some (like the Nikon TE2000) may need up to 7000 ms.
